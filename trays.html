Decorative Trays

Handcrafted wooden trays with artistic patterns, perfect for serving, organizing, or adding a decorative accent to tables and interiors.

Cultural Background & Story

Woodworking in Bangladesh dates back centuries, especially in riverine and forest-adjacent regions where artisans traditionally crafted household tools, utensils, and furniture for daily use. Decorative trays evolved from these functional beginnings, turning ordinary objects into expressions of creativity and care. Each tray is cut, shaped, polished, and decorated by hand. The patterns often draw inspiration from nature, village life, and familiar folk motifs, reflecting the environment in which the artisans live and work.

Tradition & Legacy

These trays reflect a deeply Bengali belief that beauty belongs in everyday life, not only in galleries or museums. They stand as examples of functional folk art shaped by time, experience, and cultural memory.
