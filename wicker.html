Wicker Trays & Baskets

Handwoven wicker trays and baskets, ideal for serving, storage, or home décor with a natural and rustic feel.

Cultural Background & Story

Weaving with cane, reed, and bamboo is one of the oldest living crafts in Bangladesh. In villages near rivers and wetlands, natural fibers are gathered, dried, and woven entirely by hand without the use of machines. Artisans shape each basket through experience rather than strict measurement, relying on muscle memory, instinct, and techniques learned from elders over many years.

Tradition & Legacy

This craft reflects Bangladesh’s close relationship with nature and sustainability, producing durable household items from renewable materials while preserving knowledge that has survived through generations.
