Textile Wall Art (Nakshikatha)

Handcrafted textile wall hangings with traditional motifs, perfect for wall décor and cultural interior styling.

Cultural Background & Story

Nakshi Kantha is one of Bangladesh’s most meaningful cultural expressions. Traditionally made from layers of reused cloth, each piece tells a deeply personal story of family life, love, hardship, faith, and hope. The motifs are never random. Each symbol holds meaning, and the needle becomes a language through which stories are quietly recorded.

Tradition & Legacy

Recognized internationally, Nakshi Kantha represents the emotional and historical memory of Bengali households and has been practiced mainly by rural women over centuries.
