Crochet Table Covers

Hand-crocheted table covers and runners, ideal for dining tables, coffee tables, and elegant home décor.

Cultural Background & Story

Crochet and lace-style handwork has been practiced in Bangladeshi homes for generations, often created as dowry items or family heirlooms. Each loop is formed by hand, requiring focus, time, and refined skill. Many pieces are made gradually over weeks, often during quiet moments of daily life.

Tradition & Legacy

These table covers represent quiet dedication and continuity, reflecting the often unseen labor of women artisans whose skills have shaped household traditions across generations.
