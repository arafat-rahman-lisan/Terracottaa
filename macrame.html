Macramé Mirrors

Handmade macramé wall mirrors, ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, and decorative interior wall spaces.

Cultural Background & Story

Hand-knotting techniques have existed in South Asian households for centuries, originally used to make ropes, nets, and essential household items. Modern macramé mirrors are a revival of these age-old skills, adapted into decorative art for contemporary interiors. Each mirror frame is created by tying hundreds of knots by hand, a process that demands precision, endurance, and careful attention to detail.

Tradition & Legacy

This craft reflects the adaptability of Bangladeshi artisans, who preserve traditional techniques while allowing them to evolve naturally with changing tastes and design needs.
